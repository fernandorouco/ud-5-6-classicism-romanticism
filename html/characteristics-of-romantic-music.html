From Classicism to Romanticism

Characteristics of Romantic music

Music serving the feelings

Romantic music is based on the exaltation of emotions. In contrast to Classical regularity, composers now seek expressiveness, contrast, and intensity. Melodies are freer, harmony is enriched with chromaticism and changes of key, and music aims to deeply move the listener. Music speaks to the heart, not the reason, and becomes the art best able to express the inner world of the human being.
